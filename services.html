Our Services

Comprehensive physical therapy treatments tailored to your individual needs. We treat a wide range of conditions with expertise and compassion.

Conditions We Treat

With over 30 years of clinical experience, we offer specialized treatment for a variety of diagnoses. Each therapy session is progressive and challenging, designed to help you achieve optimal healthfulness and return to an active lifestyle as quickly and safely as possible.

Specialized Treatment Services

Neurological Rehabilitation

Our neurological rehabilitation program provides expert care for patients recovering from strokes, traumatic brain injuries, Parkinson's disease, multiple sclerosis, and other neurological conditions. We use evidence-based techniques to help restore function, improve balance and coordination, and maximize independence in daily activities.

Treatments include:

  • Stroke recovery and rehabilitation
  • Balance and gait training
  • Neuromuscular re-education
  • Coordination and motor control exercises
  • Functional training for activities of daily living
  • Fall prevention programs

Orthopedic Physical Therapy

Whether you're recovering from surgery or managing a chronic condition, our orthopedic therapy programs are designed to reduce pain, restore mobility, and help you return to your normal activities. We treat all musculoskeletal conditions with personalized care plans.

We specialize in:

  • Total joint replacements (hip, knee, shoulder)
  • Post-surgical rehabilitation
  • Fracture recovery
  • Arthritis management
  • Tendonitis and bursitis
  • Rotator cuff injuries
  • Ligament and tendon repairs

Spine & Pain Management

Chronic and acute pain can significantly impact your quality of life. Our comprehensive pain management programs address neck pain, back pain, shoulder pain, and other musculoskeletal conditions using manual therapy, therapeutic exercises, and education.

Treatment for:

  • Lower back pain and sciatica
  • Neck pain and headaches
  • Shoulder pain and impingement
  • Herniated or bulging discs
  • Spinal stenosis
  • Postural dysfunction
  • Chronic pain conditions

Pediatric Physical Therapy

Our pediatric physical therapy services help children with developmental delays, congenital conditions, and injuries reach their full potential. We work closely with families to provide compassionate, age-appropriate care in a fun and encouraging environment.

We help children with:

  • Developmental delays
  • Balance and coordination deficits
  • Walking difficulties and gait abnormalities
  • Muscle tone issues (high or low tone)
  • Cerebral palsy
  • Assessment for bracing and assistive devices
  • Family training for home exercises and daily activities

Work-Related Injuries

We provide comprehensive treatment for work-related injuries and workers' compensation cases. Our goal is to help you recover fully and return to work safely and efficiently.

Common workplace injuries we treat:

  • Repetitive strain injuries
  • Carpal tunnel syndrome
  • Lower back injuries
  • Lifting-related injuries
  • Shoulder and upper extremity injuries
  • Post-injury functional capacity evaluations
  • Ergonomic assessments and training

Sports Rehabilitation

Athletes require specialized care to return to their sport safely and at peak performance. Our sports rehabilitation programs address sports-specific injuries and include functional training to prevent re-injury.

Sports injuries we treat:

  • ACL and other ligament injuries
  • Meniscus tears
  • Rotator cuff injuries
  • Tennis elbow and golfer's elbow
  • Shin splints and stress fractures
  • Ankle sprains
  • Sport-specific conditioning and training

Balance & Fall Prevention

Falls can be serious, especially for older adults. Our balance and fall prevention programs assess your risk factors and provide targeted exercises to improve stability, strength, and confidence in walking.

Program includes:

  • Comprehensive balance assessment
  • Vestibular rehabilitation
  • Strength and flexibility training
  • Gait training
  • Home safety evaluation
  • Assistive device training

Our Treatment Approach

We use a variety of evidence-based treatment techniques to achieve the best outcomes for our patients. Every treatment plan is customized to your specific condition, goals, and lifestyle.

Manual Therapy

Hands-on techniques including joint mobilization, soft tissue massage, and stretching to reduce pain and improve mobility.

Therapeutic Exercise

Customized exercise programs to restore strength, flexibility, endurance, and function.

Neuromuscular Re-education

Specialized techniques to retrain movement patterns and improve motor control.

Gait Training

Assessment and treatment to improve walking patterns, balance, and safety.

Functional Training

Task-specific exercises that prepare you for real-world activities and movements.

Patient Education

Teaching you about your condition and providing strategies for self-management and prevention.
