Our Team

DC

David T. Clawson, M.P.T.

Physical Therapist / Owner

David T. Clawson earned his Bachelor's degree in Pre-Physical Therapy from California Lutheran University in 1988 and subsequently his Master's degree in Physical Therapy from Chapman University/Children's Hospital of Los Angeles in 1990.

He has practiced in the Sacramento region for over 30 years, including seven years in skilled nursing care as a Director of Rehabilitation. David has taught at both American River and Sacramento City Colleges and participated in establishing Sutter General's Transitional Care Center.

His extensive experience and commitment to excellence have made Clawson Physical Therapy a trusted name in the community. David's approach combines clinical expertise with genuine compassion for his patients' well-being.

DL

Debra Lamb

Physical Therapist Assistant

Debra Lamb graduated from Sacramento City College in 1999, earning her Associate's Degree in Physical Therapy. She has worked in acute care as well as outpatient facilities in both California and Washington for over 20 years.

Her diverse background in various healthcare settings has given her a comprehensive understanding of patient needs across different conditions and age groups. Debra is known for her gentle approach, attention to detail, and ability to make patients feel comfortable during their therapy sessions.

She brings warmth, professionalism, and clinical skill to every patient interaction, helping individuals achieve their rehabilitation goals through personalized care and encouragement.

Our Approach

Patient-Centered Care

Every patient receives an individualized treatment plan based on their specific condition, goals, and lifestyle. We take the time to listen and understand your unique needs.

Evidence-Based Practice

Our treatments are grounded in the latest research and clinical best practices. We continuously update our skills to provide you with the most effective care.

Compassionate Environment

We create a welcoming, supportive atmosphere where patients feel comfortable and encouraged throughout their recovery journey.

Comprehensive Assessment

We conduct thorough evaluations to identify the root cause of your condition and develop the most appropriate treatment strategy.

Progressive Therapy

Each session builds on the last, with challenging yet achievable goals that help you steadily improve and regain function.

Education & Prevention

We empower you with knowledge about your condition and teach strategies to prevent future injuries and maintain your health.
