Comprehensive Physical Therapy Care

Clawson Physical Therapy is an outpatient practice with clinics located in Sacramento, CA and Elk Grove, CA. We offer patients a comfortable, enthusiastic, and accessible therapy environment. With Sacramento and Elk Grove locations, we serve clients from Folsom, Fair Oaks, Carmichael, Citrus Heights, Arden Oaks, Roseville, Rocklin, Granite Bay, Lincoln, Sacramento, and Elk Grove.

Our facilities are professional and well equipped. Patients feel right at home with our friendly and compassionate staff and the care we provide.
