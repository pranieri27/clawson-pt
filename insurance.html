Understanding Your Coverage

What to Expect

Insurance coverage for physical therapy varies by plan. Most plans cover physical therapy services, but may have copayments, coinsurance, or deductibles that apply. We will verify your benefits before your first visit and explain:

  • Your copayment or coinsurance amount
  • Whether your deductible has been met
  • How many visits your plan covers per year
  • Whether you need a referral from your physician
  • Any pre-authorization requirements

Typical Visit Costs

The cost of physical therapy varies based on your specific treatment needs. For self-pay patients, our typical visit fee is approximately $95 per session. We will provide you with a detailed estimate before beginning treatment.

Financial Policy

Payment is expected at the time of service unless other arrangements have been made in advance. If you have insurance, you are responsible for paying any copayment, coinsurance, or deductible at each visit. You are also responsible for any charges not covered by your insurance plan.

Important Information

  • Insurance Verification: While we will verify your benefits, insurance coverage is ultimately a contract between you and your insurance company. You are responsible for understanding your coverage.
  • Pre-Authorization: Some insurance plans require pre-authorization. We will help you obtain this, but you are ultimately responsible for ensuring authorization is obtained.
  • Outstanding Balances: Unpaid balances are your responsibility. We will bill your insurance, but any remaining balance after insurance payment is due within 30 days.
  • Cancellation Policy: Please provide at least 24 hours notice if you need to cancel or reschedule an appointment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a prescription or referral?

In California, you do not need a prescription to see a physical therapist. However, some insurance plans may require a referral from your physician. We can help you determine what your plan requires.

Will my insurance cover physical therapy?

Most insurance plans cover physical therapy, but coverage varies by plan. We will verify your benefits and explain your coverage before your first visit.

How many visits will I need?

The number of visits depends on your specific condition and goals. Most patients attend 2-3 times per week for several weeks. We will discuss your treatment plan and estimated duration at your initial evaluation.

What if I don't have insurance?

We offer competitive self-pay rates. Please call us to discuss payment options and potential payment plans.

Questions About Insurance or Payment?

Our friendly staff is here to help answer your questions and verify your insurance benefits.
